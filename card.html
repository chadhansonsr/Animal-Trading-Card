Cheetah

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds up to 70 miles per hour!

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx Jubatus
  • Average Length: 1.1 - 1.4 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 12 years
  • Habitat: Safari Grasslands

The cheetah is a fast and agile big cat known for its incredible speed and distinctive black tear stripes on its face. It is the fastest land animal, reaching speeds of up to 70 mph (113 km/h). Cheetahs primarily inhabit grasslands and open plains in Africa.
